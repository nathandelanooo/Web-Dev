Its time to know more about F1

Formula 1 (F1) is the world's premier single-seater, open-wheel auto racing series, featuring a season of high-speed races called Grands Prix held on circuits worldwide. Ten teams of two drivers each compete to win both a drivers' and a constructors' (team) championship.
